Overview

W3B D1V3R is a fast-paced Vampire-Survivors-style game developed during a 48-hour game jam. The player travels through a time bubble to the past to stop a deadly virus from destroying the internet. The project focused on rapid iteration, tight controls, and delivering a complete, playable experience under extreme time constraints.

My Contributions

  • Implemented core player movement and input handling
  • Built scoring and progression mechanics
  • Integrated UI and feedback systems to support moment-to-moment gameplay
  • Collaborated closely with designers and artists during rapid iteration

Design Constraints & Solutions

  • Prioritized clarity and responsiveness over feature depth due to time limits
  • Designed systems to be flexible enough for late-stage design changes
  • Scoped mechanics aggressively to ensure the game was stable and shippable

Key Takeaways

  • Clear communication is critical under severe time pressure
  • Simple systems with strong feedback outperform complex mechanics in jams
  • Early playtesting drives better decisions than upfront design documents
